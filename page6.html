Now what basic skills are aquired

Now that you have three important, basic skills on photography, it is important that you are able to combine them in order to take the best pictures possible.

Aperture is important because it allows you to adjust the amount of light that is let into your camera. Depth of field is crucial because you must know the the aspects of it when focusing on a subject. Exposure of the image is also very important becasue without the correct exposure, your photo will not come out right. These are just three of the numerous important factors of photography, but as a beginner, these three will certainly help you get on your feet.
