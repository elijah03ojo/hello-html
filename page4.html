Learning Photography as a beginner

This is a basic and beginner's guide to photography

Photography is not the only way to capture the world, but it certainly is one of the most effective. Look no further than the nearest social media feed, news station, magazine article, or book cover to see it...photographs have power. If you want to harness that power and learn how to take the best possible photos, welcome to Photography Basics The Beginners Guide. This comprehensive tutorial explains the fundamental concepts you should know about photography from start to finish.
