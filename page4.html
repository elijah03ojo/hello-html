Exposure

Exposure is an Important Part of Photography

In photography, exposure is the amount of light per unit area reaching a photographic film or electronic image sensor, as determined by shutter speed, lens aperture and scene luminance. Exposure is measured in lux seconds, and can be computed from exposure value and scene luminance in a specified region.

It is going to take a while to understand how exposure fully works. Witht the correct learning, and perseverity, this aspect of photograpy can be learned.
