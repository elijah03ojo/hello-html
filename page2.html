Your First Photos

Before taking photos, it is important to make sure that you have the right camera that works best for you.

A DSLR, a Camcorder, and a point and shoot camera are all examples of camera choices that might be right for you. It is important to do research so that the camera you are buying fulfills your needs.

There are several components to photography that will be reviewed in this segment. Aperture, exposure, and depth of field are three components that will be focused on. With these three basic skills in photography, you will be better equipped to take photos on your own.
