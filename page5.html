Depth of Field

Depth of field is key to takingg closeups

For many cameras, depth of field is the distance between the nearest and the farthest objects that are in acceptably sharp focus in an image. The depth of field can be calculated based on focal length, distance to subject, the acceptable circle of confusion size, and aperture.

Depth of field is cruicial when taking portrait photos. If you do not have the correct depth of field, the photos that you try to take will not come out correctly. Also with the correct bokeh, the blurred out portion of your picture, your depth of field shots will turn out amazing.
